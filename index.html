Tour Highlights

Join us for an unforgettable journey through the western arm of the East African Rift Valley. Experience nature, culture, and adventure in one incredible trip!

  • Visit 53 crater lakes including the famous Twin Lakes Kyema & Kamweru known for the iconic bond strengthening as stated culturally, Lake Nkugute (Lake Africa) with its stunning Africa map shape, and Lake Katinda & Mirambi for the couples.
  • Explore 12 hanging valleys, 3 gorges, and straight hilltops with stunning panoramic views.
  • Traverse the Western Arm of the East African Rift Valley.
  • Relax and enjoy top resorts: Kyangabi Crater Resort, Kingfisher Lodge, and Parkview Safari Lodge and the twinlakes Safari lodge.
